Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if it rains on the day of my event? ▼
We monitor the weather closely. If rain is forecasted, you can reschedule your event for free or receive a full refund if cancelled 24 hours in advance.
How far in advance should I book? ▼
We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for weekends. However, we often have last-minute availability, so check our store!
Is delivery and setup included? ▼
Yes! Delivery and professional setup are included for all locations within our standard service area.
